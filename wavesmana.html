The Second Homecoming

Coming home to Hong Kong again after a year's absence

November 1, 2015 Jane Doe

It has been almost a year since I have been back to Hong Kong. It's strange. I felt as though I was gone only yesterday yet at the same time so many has changed that I feel I have been gone nearly for twenty years. Time passes very fast here, and things happen even faster. If I were to compare Orange County to HK, Orange County would be like Valhala. Time passes so slow in the OC yet so many has happened other the years that has changed the whole dynamic of the community.

I haven't talked to many of my friends for a long time, some I have managed to stay in touch, others have simply become ghosts on my social media.
